ABOUT
THE FIRM

In 2009

Kurt Meachum and Jerry Philips teamed up to found and launch Philips & Meachum Public Affairs, a full-service public affairs and strategic governmental relations firm located in Austin, Texas. In 2023, Jaclyn Uresti joined the firm and together Philips, Meachum and Uresti utilize their over sixty years of combined business, political and legislative experience to provide strategic advice, messaging and communications to Philips & Meachum clients, developing unique and comprehensive plans tailored to effectively advocate and solve their client’s most challenging problems.
